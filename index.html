Frequently Asked
Questions

What is an NFT?

An NFT, or non-fungible token, is a unique digital asset that represents ownership of a specific item, whether digital or physical, and is recorded on a blockchain

What can I use NFTs for?

NFT Use Cases: Digital Art, Gaming, Music, Real-World, Certificates, Memberships, Brand Rewards, Web3 Domains Charity, Metaverse

What is the difference between an NFT and cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrencies (like Bitcoin) are fungible (interchangeable), while NFTs are non-fungible (unique and indivisible).

How much is an NFT worth?

The value of an NFT is highly subjective and can range from a few dollars to millions, depending on factors like rarity, utility, creator fame, and market demand
